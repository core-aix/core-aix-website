aGiTrack is a tool developed by the CORE-AIx Lab to help track, organize, and reason about work involving agentic AI systems.

As AI agents become more capable, the hard part is no longer only getting an agent to produce an output. The harder operational challenge is understanding what the agent did, how work progressed, where decisions were made, and what evidence supports the final result. This is especially important for research workflows, software development, evaluation, and collaborative projects where reliability and accountability matter.

aGiTrack is designed as part of CORE-AIx's broader mission: building collaborative, optimized, reliable, and efficient next-generation AI. The tool provides a dedicated place for following agent-oriented activity and making progress easier to inspect, revisit, and communicate.

For CORE-AIx, aGiTrack is also a practical research artifact. It reflects our interest in reliable agentic AI systems that can support real work without becoming opaque. Tools like this help us study not only model outputs, but also the workflows, traces, and coordination patterns that make agentic systems useful in practice.

Visit the tool here: [https://agitrack.core-aix.org](https://agitrack.core-aix.org).
